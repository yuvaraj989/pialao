THE KEY TO MANAGING TOO MUCH WEBSITE CONTENT.

About This Webinar

It's easy to get bogged down in too much website content, especially when multiple people have access to your library's website and they all the ability to create new content. We also understand that managing content often becomes even more complicated because you may be using a site that's been accruing content for several years.

That's why you'll love this webinar. We'll share some of the techniques we've used to help organizations, from large content providers to public universities, wrangle websites that have over 5,000 unique pages into a nice, neat, and strategic content utopia.

We'll look at common user behavior, design trends, and technology to help understand how users find content. Then we'll equip you with the tools and techniques to create an effective content management plan. At the end of this webinar, you'll be ready to upgrade your library website's so it better engages patrons, decreases unnecessary inquiries, and effectively communicates your library's diverse service offerings.

If you've been struggling with your content and website, then this is a can't miss. We're so confident you'll love this workshop, we'll happily refund your ticket if, for any reason, you don't

  • How patrons use websites and find content
  • Leveraging Patron behaviour in our content strategy
  • Protecting our content strategy
